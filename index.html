Photonic Core

Traditional silicon chips are hitting the "Thermal Wall." Our core bypasses electron resistance entirely, computing at the speed of light.

Zero Resistivity

Photons do not collide like electrons. They travel through our waveguides with near-zero energy loss to heat, enabling sustained peak performance.

THz Bandwidth

By using multiple wavelengths (WDM), our core can process data at Terahertz frequencies across a single lane, bypassing traditional bottlenecks.

Speed of Light

Latency is reduced to the physical minimum. Signal propagation happens at nearly 300,000 km/s within the circuit pathways.

Passive Modulation

Optical computing allows for certain logic operations to occur passively as light moves through geometry, requiring no active power draw.

Spatial Dimensions.

Our proprietary 3D stacking technology allows waveguides to cross over each other without interference, enabling integration densities previously thought impossible.

Vertical Integration

Unlike electrical vias that cause latency and heat, our optical vertical couplers move light between layers with 99.8% efficiency.

Dense Interconnects

With over 12 stacked layers of photonic circuitry, the HOLO PIC achieves the compute density of a data center rack on a single chip.

Secure Envelope

Securing intelligence at the physical layer. By utilizing the unique properties of light, we've created a chip that is invisible to traditional interception.

Evanescent Protection

Our waveguides are designed with protective cladding that triggers an immediate logic-shutdown if the field is disturbed by a physical probe.

Zero EM Leakage

Electronics leak electromagnetic signatures. Photons do not. Our chips operate in complete radio-silence, immune to side-channel attacks.

Quantum Resilience

Data is routed through non-repeating optical pathways, making physical interception functionally impossible without destroying the signal.

Physical Integrity

The atomic precision of our PIC fabrication serves as a unique hardware fingerprint, preventing unauthorized cloning or tampering.
