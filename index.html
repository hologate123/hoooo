HOLO is replacing electrons with photons. By utilizing light waves to process information, we bypass the physical limitations of traditional silicon, enabling a new era of frictionless intelligence.

The Core

Light over Electrons

Traditional electronic chips rely on the movement of electrons through copper, which creates resistance and energy loss. Photonic chips guide light through microscopic waveguides, processing data without the physical friction that slows down modern computing.

Sustainability

Thermal Liberation

Heat is the primary enemy of performance. Because photons do not generate heat as they travel, HOLO architecture eliminates the thermal wall, reducing data center energy footprints while allowing for unprecedented processing density.

Architecture

Parallelism by Design

Our chips utilize Wavelength Division Multiplexing, sending thousands of different colors of light through a single optical path. Each color carries its own independent data stream, allowing for massive parallel processing in a single circuit.
